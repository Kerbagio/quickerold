# About QuickER

## Our Mission

QuickER helps you reach the fastest hospital using live traffic and routing. It's not a substitute for professional medical advice or emergency services.

## Important Safety Information

**If you are in immediate danger, call local emergency numbers:**

- **United States & Canada**: 911
- **United Kingdom**: 999  
- **European Union**: 112
- **Australia**: 000

## What We Do

QuickER provides real-time routing assistance to emergency care facilities by:

- Analyzing current traffic conditions
- Calculating live travel times to nearby hospitals
- Filtering facilities by specialty (cardiac, pediatric, maternity, etc.)
- Providing turn-by-turn navigation to the optimal facility

## What We Don't Do

- Provide medical advice or diagnosis
- Replace emergency services
- Store your personal location data
- Make treatment recommendations

## Privacy & Data

We use your location only to compute routes. We don't store precise coordinates or personal information. All data processing happens locally on your device when possible.

## Technology Credits

QuickER is powered by:

- **Mapbox**: Map tiles and routing services
- **OpenStreetMap**: Geographic data
- **Real-time Traffic APIs**: Live traffic and ETA calculations
- Various healthcare facility databases for up-to-date information

## Disclaimer

This application is designed to assist with routing to emergency care facilities. It is not intended as medical advice and should not replace professional medical judgment or emergency services. Users should always prioritize calling emergency services in life-threatening situations.
